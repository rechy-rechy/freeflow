Canvas LMS
How to get your Canvas API token:
1. Open Canvas and click your profile picture (top-left corner)
2. Go to Account → Settings
3. Scroll down to "Approved Integrations"
4. Click + New Access Token
5. Give it any name (e.g. "Freeflow"), leave expiry blank, click Generate Token
6. Copy the token shown — you won't see it again — and paste it below
🔒 Freeflow only reads your calendar and assignments — it never posts or changes anything in Canvas.
